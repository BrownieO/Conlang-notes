# Letters
## Latin
To spell a vowel, prepend an R. I.e.
- ra, re, ri, ro, ru

To spell a consonant, append an E.g.
- Sh = she
- X = xe

When placed as a root, spell the Latin value, plus an e. E.g.
- X-axis = kse exeon

## Non-latin
Non-latin letters are spelled like in their language.
